Foodak

The next generation of restaurant management. App where you can order,track and pay through it!

The app is written in MVVM-C Pattern and built-in network serivce. Easy to use mobile interface, handles unit list management, map mode to save your location and order to it; monitoring the precise location of the driver. Notification management, receive and view notifications in the application. The most diverse restaurants in Qatar! Over 1000. filter by restaurant or item so you decide before the movie end. On top of that, you can pay through the app by multiple ways.

Kulud

Kulud is a mobile user friendly app that offers healthcare.

The app is written in MVVM-Coordinator pattern. Lists of medications and products are classified into main categories. you can filter to find items faster. Use map to locate all branches and what is near you. Snap & Submit, you can easily upload prescriptions and insurance claims. Pay through the app. In messaging feature to contact support if help is needed.

IDM

mobile app that allow IDM users to manage multiple accounts and renew services.

App is written in MVVM pattern , using Alamofire & moya for network services. It allows IDM subscribers to manage multiple internet accounts from a single or multiple mobile phones or tablets. The user can at any time check the monthly trafic consumption, renew subscriptions, manage additional services, contact support, receive notifications and stay up-to-date with IDM's news & offers.

Ehteraz

Daily healthy tips to know how to deal and react to the virus through push notifications. support contact 24/7.

MN Atelier Styling & Shopping

The app is written in MVVM-C pattern. MN Atelier is an online styling platform that connects users with professional stylists to create personalized outfits based on their preferences and needs. The platform is designed to provide a convenient and affordable way for fashion lovers to get styling advice and tips from expert stylists without leaving their homes. Users can shop by requesting custom looks, ready-made looks, as well as by brand or category. MN Atelier features a rich mix of emerging luxury designer brands that focus on slow fashion.
